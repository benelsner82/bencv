\hypertarget{research-interests}{%
\section{Research Interests}\label{research-interests}}

My research interests lie at the intersection of labour economics,
international economics, and environmental economics. I currently pursue
three strands of research: \medskip

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\item
  impact of \textbf{migration} on the sending and receiving countries,
\item
  impact of the \textbf{environment} (especially pollution) on human
  capital formation,
\item
  the effect of \textbf{peers} and teachers on human capital,
\end{enumerate}
